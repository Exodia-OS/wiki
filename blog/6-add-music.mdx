:::note[bspmonitors]
you should add your music to `~/Music` directory
:::

- Open up The Terminal and run
  - ```bash
        ncmpcpp
        # OR (only for Exodia OS)
        M
    ```
- press <kbd>2</kbd> to open **Song Browser**
- In browser tab, press <kbd>u</kbd> to update the **Music Database**
- press <kbd>SPACE</kbd> to add the songs in your **Playlist**
